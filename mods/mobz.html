The MobZ mod adds a bunch of new mobs to the game which are hard to fight and will make the minecrafter life more challenging. There are Creatures which will spawn during the day so keep caution and get well equipped to fight against them! Advancements will show you which creatures you´ve already killed and go to the Badlands to encounter the greatest of all Zombies!

Most of the MobZ Bosses spawn naturally in the Mesa Biome also known as the Badlands Biome. But there is the Pillager Boss which is summon able and through him you can get the Wither Staff which you need to summon the Withender.

First you have to get all three parts of the Pillager Totem. Two of them you can get from the Archer/Bowman and the Zombie/Spider Mage. The third part is just a cutout of wood. You will need a sacrifice knife to start the ritual. Then have to put all three totem parts together and the knife has to be bloody enough to conjure up the spirits but hurry up, the blood on your knife will dry out quickly. It will take 5 hearts to get enough blood flowing and then hit the middle part with the knife to start the process. You will see some smoke coming out of the totem which indicates all went right (or wrong cause you really summoned the pillager boss?!). After a few seconds he will appear...

If you somehow managed to take this boss down, he will probably drop the Staff which you can use to summon the Withender. Just build a statue as you would do for the Wither but instead of the wither skulls, use enderman heads. Sneak and right click on the middle head of the statue and ...

MobZ is highly configurable. Just load the mod and it will generate a config file. You will find the file in the config folder under the name "mobz.json5". Just edit it with a code editor (Example: Standard Windows Editor). If you have Mod Menu installed, you can just edit the settings while playing minecraft.

Open the ModMenu "Mods" tab and click on the gear wheel in the right top corner. It will open the config screen just as shown here. Turn specific mob spawns on and off, change the strength of the monsters through the life/attack settings and control the spawn rate of every single mob.
