The MobZ mod adds a bunch of new mobs to the game which are hard to fight and make the minecrafter life more challenging. There are Creatures which will spawn during the day so keep caution and get well equipped to fight against them! Advancements will show you which creatures you´ve already killed and go to the Badlands to encounter the greatest of all Zombies!

Most of the MobZ Bosses spawn naturally in the Mesa Biome also known as Badlands Biome. But there is the Pillager Boss which is summon able and through him you can get the Wither Staff which you need to summon the Withender.

First you have to get all three parts of the Pillager Totem. Two of them you can get from the Archer/Bowman and the Zombie/Spider Mage. The third part is just a cutout of wood. Put all three together, get an Weird Ingot and use it on the middle part of the totem and keep caution, the Pillager Boss will probably kill you! If you somehow managed to take this boss down, he will probably drop the Staff which you can use to summon the Withender. Just build a statue as you would do for the Wither but instead of the wither skulls, use enderman heads. Sneak and right click on the middle head of the statue and ...

Burn a gold ingot, cut a stripped log and put all 3 totem parts together

MobZ is highly configurable. Just load the mod and it will generate a config file. You will find the file in the config folder under the name "mobz.json5". Just edit it with a code editor (Example: Standard Windows Editor). If you have Mod Menu installed, you can just edit the settings while playing minecraft.

Open the ModMenu "Mods" tab and click on the gear wheel in the right top corner. It will open the config screen just as shown here. Turn specific mob spawns on and off, change the strength of the monsters through the life/attack settings and control the spawn rate of every single mob.
