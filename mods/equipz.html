EquipZ is a mod which adds fancy weapons with different effects to Minecraft. Most of the weapons has there own effect on the player or on the enemy. Craft a new set of armor to get stronger and become the greatest warrior of the whole Minecraft universe!

Huge thanks to Zoom31 for the designs! Axes and different types of swords will be of great help to defeat all your enemies.
