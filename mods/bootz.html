BootZ is a small mod which adds 4 tiers of Speed Boots to support you exploring the world of minecraft faster. You will get so fast you even leave a trail of fire behind you!

Tier List

Boots Durability Protection Effect
Iron Tier 143 1 (0,5) 20% Speed I
Gold Tier 195 2 (1) 40% Speed II
Diamond Tier 391 3 (1,5) 60% Speed III
Netherite Tier 437 4 (2) 60% Speed III

The stronger you get, the better your shoes should be!
