Hi and welcome to the official ModZ Wiki. My name is Micha aka Globox, I´m a mechanical engineer student from Germany. I don´t know how, but I just got interested in coding, especially in minecraft modding. I have decided to join the fabric community and write my mods with the fabric toolchain because when I first got interested in it, there weren´t mods for the newest Minecraft version and at the same time a playable optifine version except for the fabric modlauncher. I have been playing Minecraft for a while (probably spent too much lifetime in it xD), which is how I get my ideas for these mods. As time goes on I keep expanding, adding things that I either feel the game needs or I just want. I plan to keep developing these mods as well as do my best to keep up with vanilla releases. For you to know, this wiki is only for the newest version of my mods


There are no plans to develop Forge versions of my mods!
Modpack Permission: Feel free to use any of my mods in modpacks :D
Ideas: Ideas and suggestions are very welcomed, post them in the comment section or write me a message on CurseForge/Discord :)
Questions: Just write me a message and I will answer it as soon as possible
Bug Report: Just report bugs at the github page
I really love to hear what you think about my mods and I am counting on your feedback. I read and answer all the comments (except if someone is asking for a forge version)
